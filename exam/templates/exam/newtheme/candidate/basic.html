AICP Candidate Pilot Program Enrollment Overview

The AICP Candidate Pilot Program offers emerging planners the opportunity to get an early start on the path to certification by the American Institute of Certified Planners. Students enrolled in and graduates of programs accredited by the Planning Accreditation Board (at the time of their graduation) are eligible to participate in the AICP Candidate Pilot Program.

These pages will guide you through the enrollment process. You will be asked to:

  • Verify your email address and APA chapter
  • Tell APA whether you are a student or a graduate
  • Enter and verify information about your educational status
  • Read and verify that you have read the AICP Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct
  • Review and approve your enrollment application
  • Pay a $20 enrollment fee

Please read the AICP Candidate Pilot Program Guide before you begin.

You may complete your enrollment or stop and save it part way through and return later. To update or check the status of your enrollment in the AICP Candidate Pilot Program, go to the AICP STATUS section of My APA.
