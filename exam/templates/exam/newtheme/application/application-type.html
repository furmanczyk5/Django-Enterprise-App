AICP Certification APPLICATION

The AICP Certification Examination tests the knowledge and skills of members eligible for membership in AICP. To become eligible for certification, a member must submit an application that demonstrates that he or she has obtained the required combination of education and professional planning experience.

The application consists of:

  • Section 1: Describe and verify educational history
  • Section 2: Describe and verify employment history
  • Section 3: Respond to eligibility criteria (Describe how your work experience fulfills APA's definition of professional planning experience.)
  • Section 4: Read and verify you have read the AICP Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct
  • Section 5: Application Summary and Review

You may complete your application all at once or save it and return to it later.

Please thoroughly read the AICP Guide Part 1: Certification Application before you begin your application.
